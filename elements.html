Comparison of Jobs

Whilst our chosen careers are in radically different IT fields, they share many similarities. They all require a strong background in teamwork, both working within a single team and with other teams in the same company. They require strong communication skills, both within the immediate work environment and with customers/potential customers. They also require at least a basic understanding of common programing languages.

The key differences in our chosen careers are in the specifics of what’s required, in the case of games development and machine learning a strong understanding of specific programming languages is required. An AWS Engineer needs to have a good back ground in many different operating systems as well as strong understanding of web architecture. Someone in technical support will need to be able strong background in the field they are supporting to deal with clients who might not have an IT background.
